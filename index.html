Studio Cosplay promotes community through the art of costuming by providing workspace, opportunity and education.

Studio Cosplay is a non-profit organization (501(c)3 status pending) located in the DC metro Area that will be offering studio makerspace in 2015 to cosplayers who need a place to work on their projects. We will provide various costume and prop-making equipment and will offer green screen space to photographers who would like to come in and work with cosplayers. Member subscription tiers will be available for use of the workshop.
