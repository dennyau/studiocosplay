Mascot Contest!

Ladies and Gentlemen,

Thank you for all the love and support you have given us throughout this year! Now that we have our 501(c)(3) and our official logo, Studio Cosplay would like to announce a very important contest.

Help us design our Studio Cosplay MASCOT!

If you are a professional designer or simply a professional fan we want to see what you can do! We are looking for a new MASCOT. Our vision is to have an adorable dragon that can be displayed on various publications wearing costumes and working hard in the studio.

The Winner will receive a $100 Amazon gift card (not to mention bragging rights and our love and gratitude).

Please send your submissions to contactstudiocosplay@gmail.com no later than 11:59 PM EST on December 31, 2014. Winners will be announced in the first weeks of January 2015.

We look forward to seeing your designs!

Official Rules:

  1. Rastor entries should be .jpg, png., .psd, .pdf, or layered .tif format no larger than 20MB. Preferred minimum of 200dpi (600dpi is better) .psd or layered .tif format.

  2. Any technique or medium may be used, as long as the final submission is in the specified digital format. If you use stock photos, art, etc., make sure it is either public domain or that you own the rights to use it.

  3. Entrants must be 18 years of age and must include real name (we can’t give you a prize if we don’t know your name!). If you would like us to use a nick/handle or professional name in contest announcements, please indicate this on your entry (and include the name!).
